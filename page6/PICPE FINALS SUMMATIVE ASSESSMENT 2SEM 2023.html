Tri-people of Mindanao

The National Commission on Indigenous Peoples (NCIP’s) Resolution no. 08-009-2021, dated 2 March 2021, denounced the use of the term ‘Lumad’ to refer to indigenous peoples of Mindanao. The NCIP’s resolution claims that indigenous leaders are ‘clamoring’ for an end to the use of the name ‘Lumad’ to refer to the indigenous peoples of Mindanao. The resolution states that the ‘emergence’ of the name ‘Lumad’ is ‘marred’ by its association with the Communist Party of the Philippines-New People’s Army-National Democratic Front (CPP-NPA-NDF). The resolution decries how the name ‘Lumad’ is ‘not an indigenous term.’ (https://www.facebook.com/ntfelcac/posts/read-commission-en-banc-resolution-no-08-009-2021-series-of-2021-a-resolution-de/450826246355765/)

Do you think that the NCIP is right to decry the name ‘lumad’ as not an indigenous term?
